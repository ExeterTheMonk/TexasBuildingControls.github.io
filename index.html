Plan & Spec

Texas Building Controls is committed and ready to go to work with customers throughout the lengthy Plan and Spec market process; including but not limited to: Narrative Budgetary pricing, Design and Development, Progress Schematics, Final Bid Sets and Value Engineering.

Design & Build

Texas Building Controls prides itself on listening to what you as a customer are striving for. We will work together, make suggestions as needed and ultimately create a custom solution to make your building automation hopes become reality.

Lighting Control

Texas Building Controls looks forward to the opportunity on helping you wade through the darkness that is the overwhelming lighting industry. Together we can simplify your lighting control needs while providing you a robustly performing lighting energy management package.

Analytics

With a state of the art energy dashboard, you can see where your building is misusing energy and harness the full potential of your automation system. Together; we can monitor your buildings’ natural gas usage, water usage and electrical consumption. All of this data will be in an easy to use format that can actually be understood and not be just another report that everyone ignores.

Gas Detection

Todays building codes dictate that parking structures, VRF refrigerant systems, ammonia chillers and batterie charging stations adhere to monitored and fan ventilation requirements. Texas Building Controls has experience in all the above and will be happy to help you design a solution for your building.

Access Control

Granting or denying access to any building is an important part of earning peace of mind from your building’s occupants. Card access and camera systems can be an add-on to your existing building controls or a stand-alone system. We look forward to the opportunity to discuss your security options with you.
